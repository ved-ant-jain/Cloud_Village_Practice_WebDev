Harley Kimball, also known by the handle infinitelogins, has actively participated in the hacker community for over a decade, specifically focusing on the bug bounty space for the last two years. Harley is an experienced pentester and bug bounty hunter with over eight years of technical expertise in information security. Harley is a prominent figure in the cybersecurity community, managing a well-known blog and YouTube channel that boasts over 10,000 followers. Harley actively participates in local information security meetups and conferences, including RainSec and BSides PDX. Additionally, Harley is deeply engaged with Discord communities and content creation groups, and plays a key role in organizing Live Hacking Events and HactivityCon for HackerOne.
